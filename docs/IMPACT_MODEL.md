## Impact Estimates

### 1. Time Saved

**Assumption:** A diligent retail investor manually checks:
- NSE corporate filings: 10 min/day
- Bulk/block deals: 5 min/day
- Insider trade data: 10 min/day
- Quarterly results analysis: 15 min/event (50+ companies/quarter)
- SEBI circulars: 5 min/day
- Cross-referencing all of the above: 15 min/day

**Total manual effort:** ~60 min/day per investor

**With Opportunity Radar:**
- Ingestion + analysis: Automated (0 min)
- Review curated signals: ~5 min/day
- Deep analysis on select stocks: ~5 min/stock

**Time saved per investor:** ~50 min/day = **~300 hours/year**

**At scale (1% of active traders = 350,000 users):**
- **105 million hours saved per year** across user base

---

### 2. Revenue Recovered (Missed Opportunities)

**Assumption:** The average retail investor misses 2–3 actionable signals per month due to:
- Not reading filings in time
- Missing insider buying patterns
- Not connecting quarterly results to price action

**Conservative estimate:**
- Average missed opportunity cost: ₹2,000–5,000 per signal (2–5% move on ₹1L position)
- Signals missed per year: ~30
- Revenue recovered per investor: **₹60,000–₹1,50,000/year**

**At scale (350,000 users):**
- **₹2,100 Cr – ₹5,250 Cr** in recovered value annually

**More conservative (assume 10% signal accuracy):**
- **₹210 Cr – ₹525 Cr** in recovered value annually

| Signal Type | Expected Accuracy | Reasoning |
